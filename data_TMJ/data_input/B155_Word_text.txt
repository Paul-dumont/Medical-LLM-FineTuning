Severe TMJ pain. Headaches. Severe myofascial pain. Decreased oropharyngeal airway with an AP dimension of 2 mm (normal AP dimension is 11 mm).
Hypertrophied turbinates. Nasal airway obstruction. Sleep apnea symptoms. Recommended treatment would be as follows:

Presurgical orthodontics. Align and level maxillary arch. Align and level mandibular arch. Slenderize the lower anterior teeth removing about 2 mm of tooth structure and retract. CT scan (TMJ Concepts protocol).
