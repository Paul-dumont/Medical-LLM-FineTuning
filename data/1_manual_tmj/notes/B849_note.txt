1.	Rheumatology work-up for JRA.  
2.	Presurgical orthodontics.
	a.	Align and level maxillary arch.  Tip lateral incisor roots mesially and cuspid roots 		distally to make room for interdental osteotomies.  Place brackets on the second 			molars, but do not engage the second molars in the arch wire.

	a.	Bilateral TMJ reconstruction and mandibular advancement in a counterclockwise 			direction with TMJ Concepts total joint prostheses.

	d.	Augmentation genioplasty with Medpor.
5.	Postsurgical orthodontics to refine occlusion.
