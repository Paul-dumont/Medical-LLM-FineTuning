RECOMMENDED TREATMENT
Presurgical orthodontics.
Align and level maxillary arch.  Tip laterals incisor roots mesially and cuspid roots distally to make room for interdental osteotomies.  Open up space around the upper lateral incisors to equal 1 mm around each tooth.
Align and level mandibular arch.  Use Class III elastics on the right side and Class II elastics on the left side to try and improve the asymmetry in the lower arch where the left cuspid is about 2 mm posterior to the right cuspid.  He should only need these elastics for a couple of months to correct that situation.
Surgery.
Multiple maxillary osteotomies to advance the maxilla, level it transversely and correct any transverse discrepancies.
Bilateral mandibular ramus osteotomies to advance the mandible in a slight counterclockwise direction.
Bilateral partial turbinectomies.
Nasoseptoplasty.
Removal of impacted maxillary third molars and malaligned erupted mandibular right third molar. 
Postsurgical orthodontics to refine occlusion.
